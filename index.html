Madora Lawson
Is a Seattle native with over 25 years experience as an interior designer. The sketches and spatial compositions for interior spaces she uses daily in her work greatly influence her art. She uses a variety of media: soft pastels, found objects, oils, watercolor. Small in scale, these pieces use texture, color, and multiple perspective to create a strong sense of movement.
